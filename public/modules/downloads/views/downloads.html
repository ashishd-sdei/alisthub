Sidekick

  • Sidekick is a native utility that connects to our ticketing system to smoothly run your hardware peripherals. Run Sidekick when using our website to:
  • Print thermal tickets or receipts from your connected Boca printer.
  • Trigger the opening of an APG NetPRO cash drawer after a cash sale in the box office.

Supported Hardware

Boca Thermal Printers: 300 DPI, Supports FGL, Interleaved 2 of 5 (bar codes)

APG NetPRO Cash Drawers: NetPRO Interface 470, 480, 488, and 490

Instructions

  1. Choose the appropriate installer for your computer below. The installer option in blue is compatible with your machine and should be selected.
  2. Run the installer and use the defaults.
  3. The final steps may take several minutes depending on your version of Windows.
  4. Choose to restart your computer when prompted.
